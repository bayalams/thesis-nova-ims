# Tourism Intelligence System - Test Queries

Use these questions to validate that the system is correctly retrieving and synthesizing information from the 57 connected RSS feeds.

## 1. Trend Detection & Analysis
*Test the system's ability to synthesize broad market movements.*
- "What are the current trends in Portuguese tourism based on recent news?"
- "Are there any emerging travel patterns from German tourists to Portugal?"
- "What economic factors are currently affecting tourism demand in Portugal?"
- "How is the winter season performing compared to last year?"

## 2. Competitive Intelligence
*Test coverage of Spanish news sources and comparative analysis.*
- "What is Spain doing differently in tourism marketing compared to Portugal?"
- "How is the Algarve positioned against competing Mediterranean destinations?"
- "What new tourism initiatives has Spain announced recently?"
- "Cite specific examples of competitive threats mentioned in El País."

## 3. Market-Specific Insights
*Test coverage of the 5 key source markets (UK, US, Germany, France, Spain).*
- **UK**: "What are UK travelers saying about Portugal right now?"
- **US**: "Are there any concerns or negative perceptions about Portugal in US travel media?"
- **France**: "What French tourism trade publications (like TourMaG) are reporting about Portugal?"
- **Germany**: "What are the latest travel advisories or reports from German sources like Spiegel or Süddeutsche?"

## 4. Operational & Supply Signals
*Test aviation and trade data retrieval.*
- "Are there any airline route changes or new flights to Portugal?"
- "What aviation disruptions could impact Portuguese tourism this month?"
- "What is ANA Aeroportos reporting about recently?"
- "Summarize the latest hotel opening announcements from trade press."

## 5. Policy & Institutional
*Test UNWTO, EU, and government source coverage.*
- "What are the latest UNWTO recommendations that could affect Portugal?"
- "Are there any new EU tourism regulations or sustainability initiatives?"
- "What is Eurostat showing about tourism statistics in Portugal?"

## 6. Luxury & High-Value Segment
*Test the newly added Vogue and Condé Nast feeds.*
- "How is Portugal being featured in luxury travel publications like Vogue?"
- "What high-end travel experiences in Portugal are getting media attention?"
- "List the 'best hotels' in Portugal recently mentioned by international press."

## 7. Crisis & Risk Monitoring
*Test ability to detect negative signals.*
- "Are there any negative news stories that could damage Portugal's tourism reputation?"
- "What economic challenges are tourists from key markets facing?"
- "Are there any strikes, protests, or disruptions affecting Portuguese tourism?"
